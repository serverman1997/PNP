People's National Party

Jonathan La Crette is a dedicated community leader from Grenada who is committed to serving his constituents as a father figure, mentor, educator, and coach. With years of vast professional experience and a strong investment in youth development and the well-being of his community, he is now running for office to continue working towards meaningful progress for Grenada and lifting the humanitarian standard in his constituency and the nation. With a strong spiritual background, he is a passionate and driven individual with a clear purpose and dedication to his community. His leadership is grounded in service and genuine care for the people of St. George North West and Grenada.

Jonathan passionately advocates for a "healthy democracy" that embodies sustainability and resilience for Grenada, emphasizing a visionary approach for the future. His commitment lies in strengthening our political system and institutions to ensure they are robust, transparent, inclusive, and capable of standing the test of time. Moreover, he prioritizes significant long-term investments in education, healthcare, agriculture, the economy, and rural development to secure a thriving future for all citizens.

Key Commitments

  • He will champion the establishment of well-organized and effective political organizations with a governance model that is adaptable and resilient.
  • He will elevate the policymaking process to ensure quality and empowerment remain paramount.
  • He will vehemently advocate for political leadership, both in government and our communities, to carry a semblance of authenticity and unwavering trust.
  • He will defend the institutions that serve the public with full transparency.

Top Sectorial Priorities

  • National Security
  • Education
  • Healthcare
  • Agriculture
  • Youth and Sports

Jonathan stands out as an exceptionally qualified candidate for the constituency of St. George North West and for Grenada. His Spirituality, charisma, influence, and engaging personality are intoxicating and resonate with the general population. Driven by an unwavering commitment to his people, his extensive background as an educator, coach, and mentor has provided him with the essential skills and insights needed for effective public service. Jonathan has an appealing grassroots approach towards community engagement. This hands-on experience not only deepens his understanding of community challenges but also makes him a powerful advocate for the needs of his constituents and Grenadians. Jonathan’s genuine passion for rural and community development ensures that he will always prioritize the interests of citizens, tirelessly working towards a brighter and more prosperous future for all.

You can support Jonathan’s campaign by spreading the word, volunteering, and getting involved in community activities. Stay updated with campaign events and initiatives through the website or contact Jonathan’s team directly for more ways to help.
